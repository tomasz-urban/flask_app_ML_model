About

 Thanks for stopping by!

 About myself ... probably the most difficult thing to be asked. One thing is not to get You bored with too long monologue, the other is to write enough that You would be interested in getting to know me better.

 You can call me crazy but in the age of 36 I decided to change my life completely! I had quite good job with enough pay to lead a decent life ... but it wasn't enough for me. I was looking for something to focus on for quite long time, something to spend the rest of my life with. After a thorough check, of all of the available options, I found it! Machine learning! I thought why not, I could teach machines, it would probably be easier then teaching my two daughters. Machines are not tired, doesn't have different opinion, doesn't have everything else better to do than listening to me ... and that's where my journey began ...

 From that day several months have passed, and now I know, that that's not so easy as I thought, but I'm doing my best to improve my skills every day. Since then I did couple of courses which You can check in my skills section and everyday I feel more comfortable with the decision I made. I know I'm still far from my goal and still have a lot to learn, but I will get there!

 And that's pretty much it .... if You want to know more please contact me using Contact Form!
